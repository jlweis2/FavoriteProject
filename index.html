This program runs when given a sequence of rotations and reflections of a colored square. It determines the orientation of that same square both before and after the rotations and reflections have been applied one-after-another, from left-to-right. The numbers optional to give are 1-8.

1: This will rotate all the colors counter clockwise.

2: This will rotate all the colors counter clockwise twice.

3: This Will rotate all the colors counter clockwise three times.

4: This flips the colors based on the horizontal axis.

5: This flips the colors based on the vertical axis.

6: This flips the colors on the diagonal, from upper-right to lower-left.

7: This flips the colors on the diagonal, from upper-left to lower-right.
